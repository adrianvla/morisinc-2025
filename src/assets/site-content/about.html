Mission

I am a transhumanist. I believe robotics should support humanity in every possible way, and that society should benefit from technological progress. I also believe that knowledge is a human right and should be freely accessible to all.

Technologies such as exoskeletons and advanced prosthetics can transform lives, especially for people with disabilities.

As computers approach human-level cognition and emotional understanding and outright true emotions, they could accelerate innovation, science, and society at large. Such capabilities must be developed and deployed responsibly.

Technological disruption can lead to misconceptions and fears of replacement. Preparing society for change will be essential to live in harmony with such advanced technology.
