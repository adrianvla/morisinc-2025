Overview

mCall - Choose who you want to speak to in a meeting, with an end-to-end encryption.

Each stream is peer to peer, with an end-to-end encryption.

Don't trust/don't like a peer in a specific meeting? You can outright refuse to connect to them.

You can find the source code [a href="https://github.com/adrianvla/mCall"]here[/a]

Visit [btn link="https://mcall.morisinc.net/build/"]mCall[/btn]

[img src="assets/img/mcall.webp"][/img]

Features

  • Screensharing
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Peer to peer connections
  • You can decline a connection to a peer
  • Works on mobile and desktop
  • Open source
  • Can host online classes (option to raise your hand)
